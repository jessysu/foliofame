Making Dense Sense

The dense learners pick up the target's closing price after a related article being published. They try to learn the price change since the publish day to 2 months later.

An article may say good things about a stock, while the stock performs worse since the publish day. The learners were design to capture the relationship between an article and the near-future price of a stock.

The learned "sense" from an article to a stock, or focus sense, is then being combined toward the stock. The combination also considers how each article influence all its related stock - to have room in model generalization.

That makes the dense sense of a stock.
