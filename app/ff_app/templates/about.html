About Us

FolioFame summarizes the stock data into deep, readable insights.

We aim to simplify the knowledge discovery and understanding process for human traders like us. Our analytics covers the major stocks for best timing, tips for holding, volume watch, daily moves, flux trend, windowed performance, and historical secrets. Everyday, our engine crunches over 2.5TB of data to generate the realtime U.S. stock market summaries with multi-granularity detail.
